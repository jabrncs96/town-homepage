Top three activities to do at Hermosillo

Visit the city's cathedal

Hermosillo's cathedral is a good place to hang around and have some hot churros.

Enjoy some delicious tacos

Go to any of our taco places, all of them are exceptionally delicious.

Go for a hike and workout

At Cerro de la Campana you can go for a good workout and enjoy the view.
